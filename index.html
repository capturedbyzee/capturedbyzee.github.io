About

THE PERSON
BEHIND THE LENS

Born and raised in California, Zee Chauhan is a Deaf, Afro-Asian media and communications professional now based in Berlin. A media journey that began at Deaf Film Camp in New York — producing short films alongside professional Deaf filmmakers — has grown into over a decade of work spanning filmmaking, photography, community communications, and accessibility advocacy.

Zee has collaborated with Google, Apple, and Yahoo on accessibility in film and digital experiences, and has interviewed figures including Apple CEO Tim Cook and Deaf advocate Nyle DiMarco on the future of inclusive design. Currently building Deaf community connections across Europe through social media and communications roles in Berlin.
